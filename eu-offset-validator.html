The Challenge

In last-mile operations, each delivery route requires an offset buffer — critical minutes added to account for breaks, traffic, station congestion, and weather variables.

These offsets are applied on D-1 during route sequencing and must be validated on D-0 before routes go live. Without correct offsets, driver waves clash, routes become over-compressed, and customer promises are at risk.

Manual validation across the entire EU network consumed 7 labour hours per week, was vulnerable to human error, and lacked real-time visibility for stakeholders.

Key Benefits

  • Time Savings: Reduced validation from ~1 hour +/- daily to under ~5 minutes — a ~90% efficiency improvement
  • EU-wide Scalability: One script audits 400+ stations in parallel with zero additional labor
  • Reliability: Automatic checks ensure no station is missed, eliminating oversight risk
  • Transparency: Stakeholders receive real-time notifications with details of any mismatches
  • Resource Reallocation: Enabled operations teams to redirect capacity toward higher-value decision-making and problem-solving.
