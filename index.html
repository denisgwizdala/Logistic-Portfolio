Why Companies That Replaced Workers With AI Are Quietly Reversing Course

Tesla, Klarna, IBM, Duolingo, and Taco Bell rushed to replace workers with AI, promising efficiency and massive cost savings. What followed were production failures, quality drops, and customer backlash. Most are now quietly rehiring staff and reversing their decisions. The lesson? The goal isn't replacing humans—it's building operations that combine human judgment with AI capabilities.
