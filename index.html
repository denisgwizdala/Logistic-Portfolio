About me:

I am a Supply Chain Transformation and Process Automation Specialist with 5 years’ experience driving innovation in last-mile delivery across Europe. At Amazon, I coordinated logistics for more than 400 stations while developing Python and Java automation solutions that eliminated bottlenecks, reduced manual effort, and improved operational performance at scale. My projects have delivered over 3,000 hours of manual labour savings annually, generated more than £100,000 in annual cost reductions, and achieved efficiency gains of up to 90% in key processes, while also reducing errors through the application of automated guardrails. Several of these initiatives have been piloted and successfully adopted across the EU network. I combine hands-on technical expertise with strategic supply chain knowledge, transforming complex challenges into scalable solutions that drive operational excellence and sustainable business growth.
