Introduction

Over the last 5 years in last-mile delivery and supply chain coordination, I've turned my passion for problem-solving into a relentless focus on efficiency. I identify wasted time, redundant steps, and operational bottlenecks, transforming them into streamlined processes that save hours, reduce risk, and strengthen delivery performance.

What started with simple Excel formulas grew into full Python and Java solutions — cutting manual effort, reducing errors, and helping teams focus on higher-value work. Amazon gave me the scale to prove these ideas: coordinating across 400+ stations in one of Europe's busiest and most fast-paced supply chains.

From rising delivery volumes and tighter customer expectations to manual coordination and complex workflows, I transformed operational pressures into tools that supported colleagues and delivered measurable results across the European network. Along the way, I became a point of contact for technical issues and a driver of innovation, coordinating across EU and US teams to deliver scalable, impactful solutions.

This portfolio showcases that 5-year journey — a mix of automation, decision-support, and productivity initiatives — all born out of real operational needs and a drive to make things faster, simpler, and more reliable.
