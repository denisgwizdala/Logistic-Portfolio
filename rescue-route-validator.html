The Validation Crisis

Manual Process Breaking at Scale

Routes frequently fall behind schedule across EU supply chain operations. Rescue validation—determining if backup support is needed—was critical but entirely manual.

Schedulers spent 30 minutes per validation, manually pulling data from multiple disconnected spreadsheets and internal systems. Without automated checks, validation quality varied by scheduler—risking missed routes or unnecessary rescues.

Mandatory Validation
