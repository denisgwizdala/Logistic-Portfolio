DEEP DIVE ANALYSIS
After ~1 year of data collection, comprehensive analysis revealed patterns across the EU last mile network. Station-by-station contribution percentages identified systematic operational gaps generating phantom shipments.
Analysis findings escalated to EU Process Team enabled root cause identification. Team implemented SOP changes addressing underlying operational practices—eliminating the problem at its source and generating ~£80K annual cost savings from eliminated phantom-related operational inefficiencies.
OUTCOME (2025+): Phantom generation reduced dramatically. Tool transitioned from handling ~35K annual removals to operating as guardrail for occasional outliers — proof that data-driven intervention solved the systematic failure. Combined impact: ~£99K annually (~£19K labor + ~£80K process improvement).
01

The Ghost
in the System

Phantom shipments were accumulating in routing clusters across Europe—invalid entries that remained stuck in the system. These non-existent shipments generated unnecessary routes, inflated delivery time estimates, and wasted computational resources. Operations teams spent ~20 hours weekly removing these entries manually, but without proper data collection, identifying root causes was impossible—only addressing symptoms without resolving the underlying issue.
